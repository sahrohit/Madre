About Us


Our History

Owned and operated by Martinez Family for 37 years, we take pride in the homemade goodness of our creative lunch and dinner selections, using the freshest ingredients and our uncompromising standards!
Originally established in 1951 by Rosa Diaz as a seasonal restaurant serving strictly fresh seafood, it was sold in 1953 to the Martinez family, who expanded it to a full size family restaurant.
